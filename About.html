WHO ARE WE?

We are a team of cinephiles who is on a quest to get the richest, latest, and most reliable film information and reviews.

MISSION

To collect the best film reviews, recommend movies according to your taste, and be the best cinephile friend you'll ever have.

VISION

To be come the leading film reviewer/information site best known for its swift movie recommendations

THE STORY

In an oceam of films, it takes so much time to decide which is the best to watch. Friends are waiting, family members are getting bored, and your movie marathon/sleepover plans have been consumed by your endless need to scroll

For this reason, on September 6, 2022, Justin and Mikhaela, both cinephiles, decided to design a web template with the sole purpose of imparting reliable reviews, recommendations, and updates in the fastest and easiest way possible.
